Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Usage

This project, MedScan Insight, collects and uses certain information for the sole purpose of providing a seamless and personalized experience. The following outlines the types of data I collect and how it is used:

User Registration

When users register for our service, we collect the following information:

  • Username
  • Profile Picture
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Age

This information is essential for creating and personalizing user profiles within the application.

Disease Detection

Our project may involve collecting data related to disease symptoms or health conditions, solely for the purpose of disease detection within the application. This information is processed locally within the application and is not shared or used for any external purposes.

Data Security

I take the security of your data seriously and have implemented various measures to ensure its protection:

  • All sensitive data, such as the password, is stored securely using encryption
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

User Rights

Users have the following rights regarding their data:

  • The right to access their personal information
  • The right to request the deletion of their accounts and data.

Transparency

I am committed to being transparent about data practices. This privacy policy serves as a clear guide on how user data is collected, processed, and protected.

Contact Information

For any inquiries or concerns regarding privacy practices, please contact us at linhtetwin.mm@gmail.com.
